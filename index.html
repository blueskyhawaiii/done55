Easypaisa Payment Method

Easypaisa is one of the most used mobile wallets in Pakistan. It allows users to send and receive money easily using a phone.

Deposit via Easypaisa

  • Open Easypaisa app
  • Select Send Money
  • Enter account details
  • Confirm payment

Withdraw via Easypaisa

  • Go to withdrawal section
  • Enter mobile number
  • Submit request

This method is best for beginners because it is simple and quick.

JazzCash Payment Method

JazzCash is another reliable option. It works in a similar way and is trusted by millions.

Deposit via JazzCash

  • Open JazzCash app
  • Select transfer option
  • Enter details
  • Confirm

Withdraw via JazzCash

  • Enter your JazzCash number
  • Submit request

JazzCash is perfect for users who want fast and smooth transactions.

Bank Transfer Method

Bank transfer is useful for users who deal with large amounts. It may take more time but is reliable.

  • Use bank app or branch
  • Send money to given account
  • Upload proof if needed
